Privacy Statement




We take user privacy pretty dang seriously.


Our goal is to make Blind Cat Rescue a great experience. However, as much as we love you, we are not interested in knowing everything about you. Blind Cat Rescue may collect, use, and disclose your personal information in the manner described in this privacy and cookies policy.


Here's the scoop:


If you sign up for our newsletter we ask for your name and email address. We can not send it to you with out that info. That info is stored on Mailchimp.com servers. We may download it in a file to our local computer and save it for 1 year on dropbox. We NEVER EVER share that info with anyone.

Mailchimps Policy Statement

If you buy a calendar or raffle ticket for a fundraiser, we ask for your mailing address & email address. We cannot ship it with out that info and cannot let you know that it has been shipped without your email address. That information is stored on GoDaddy.com servers until the end of the fund raiser. That info is downloaded to our computer and stored for 2 years on Dropbox.

GoDaddy Privacy Policy

If you make a donation to us on our website, thank you so much!! The payment processor is Paypal. Paypal collects the info they need to process the donation. After we thank you for your donation, the email is deleted from our mail server. The info is saved on Paypalâ€™s servers. We do download it 2 or 3 times a year. It is saved on our physical computer and the info is never shared with any one else. It is deleted after 2 years. Blind Cat Rescue does not collect, store or have access to the payment information you provide Paypal. All such info is subject the privacy policy of Paypal

Paypal Privacy Policy






COOKIE POLICY: Our website has ZERO cookies, no third party cookies, no cookies at all except the oreos in the kitchen. We do not collect or share your info with anyone for any reason.


Bottom line, we only have the info about you that we absolutely need and we share it with no one.
