Meet the Cats



We would like to introduce you to the blind, FIV & FELV+ residents of Blind Cat Rescue


Every cat may have more than one sponsor. We urge our supporters to choose whichever cat you find touches your heart the most, as they will all receive the same standard of care regardless of how many sponsors they have. It costs approximately $1000 per day to provide the cats with shelter, quality food, and medical care.

Thank you for your support in helping the cats!
